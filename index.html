Neptu is an online video game where exploration, construction, economy and combat combine in an infinite world to create a unique universe.

Explore and survive in a hostile post-apocalyptic world, battle enemy creatures and players, colonize territories, then build and develop your own villages, join other existing villages, and trade and commerce.

In Neptu, YOU choose YOUR way to play.

EXPLORE

Travel to a post-apocalyptic and infinite world where exploration is the key. Explore different regions to harvest resources, and find places of interest that can make you rich or bring you knowledge. But beware, the environment can be hostile...

FIGHT

Confront lurking dangers, track down your enemies and hunt for survival. Group up with other players to defeat the most dangerous creatures! But be careful that the hunter does not become the hunted...

BUILD

Build and join allied camps and villages to rest, haggle and prepare for your next expedition. Develop your village to make it a well-known landmark for other travelers, and to increase your notoriety at the same time. Build your own house, make it pretty and store your resources in it.

CRAFT

Use your skills to create the perfect stuff for your next expedition and gain experience in order to craft better items! Forge, cook, hunt, harvest and manufacture from the resources you collect and maybe your reputation will bring you customers...

TRADE

Go to the villages to exchange your rarest resources or buy those of other adventurers. Travel from village to village to buy and resell more prized resources. But beware not to lose your merchandise, or to lose yourself...
