Neptu is an online video game where exploration, construction, economy and combat combine in an infinite world to create a unique universe.

Explore and survive in a hostile post-apocalyptic world, battle enemy creatures and players, colonize territories, then build and develop your own villages, join other existing villages, and trade and commerce.

In Neptu, YOU choose YOUR way to play.
